  • The latter have been duc[kt]-taped with SSL/TLS: POP3S, IMAPS, FTPS...

State of the art.

Why all this failure?

  • Security wasn't a main concern at the time of their design: FTP, POP3...
  • Legacy code, algorithms, standards.(Open|Libre)SSL/TLS
  • "If it has worked once, you won't get rid of it". php, javascript SMTP.

Fortunately, new IoT standards are built with security in mind.

Security

What are the roots of IT security?

Cryptography

  • Asymmetric cryptography preferred.
  • Strong algorithms.
  • Large cryptographic keys sizes.
  • "True" Random Number Generation

Good practices

  • Strong passwords.
  • Multi-factor authentication.
  • Revocable Trust.
